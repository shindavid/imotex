It suffices to show $\sum_{n=1}^\infty n/2^n = 2$:

$$
\sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{n}{2^n} = \sum_{n=1}^\infty \sum_{k=n}^\infty \frac{1}{2^k} = \sum_{n=1}^\infty \frac{1}{2^{n-1}} = 2
$$
